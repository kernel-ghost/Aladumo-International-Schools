Facilities

Spaces designed to support curiosity, comfort, creativity, safety, and focused learning.

Our Facilities

Library

A rich collection of books, digital resources, and quiet reading spaces to foster a love for learning.

Science Labs

Fully-equipped laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology to support hands-on experimentation.

Sports Complex

Indoor and outdoor facilities for football, athletics, and other sports to encourage fitness and teamwork.

Art & Music Studios

Spaces for creative expression through music, drama, painting, and performing arts activities.

Computer Labs

Modern computer labs with high-speed internet and software tools for digital literacy and coding skills.

Healthy Canteen

Clean and hygienic dining areas providing nutritious meals and snacks for students and staff.

Built for Learning

Our facilities support active learning, independent study, creativity, fitness, and collaboration. Each space is designed to help students feel comfortable, confident, and ready to participate.

From classroom learning to outdoor activities, our environment helps students discover their strengths and develop habits that prepare them for life beyond examinations.
